Cutting Bowl

~280 cal · 22g protein · 3 min
  • 170g plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup strawberries sliced
  • 15g almonds chopped (about 12)
  • Optional cinnamon
Want sweeter? 1 tsp honey adds 20 cal.

Healthy Sandwich

~400 cal · 30g protein · 5 min
  • 2 slices whole grain or sourdough bread
  • 100g sliced turkey breast
  • 1 slice cheese
  • Big handful spinach or lettuce
  • Sliced tomato and cucumber
  • 1 tsp mustard or 1/4 mashed avocado (skip mayo)
Cutting tips: Turkey beats ham. Mustard saves 100 cal vs mayo. Pile on greens.
